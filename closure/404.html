Letter of Closure

By Poley Magik on April 6, 2020

This is a message to any GoAnimate Wrapper users who bothered to check the network tab when our service inevitably closed. Before you get angry at us, please read the whole story.

Hello, I am Poley Magik, I am the guy who made the asset store for GoAnimate Wrapper, and today, I am announcing the complete cease in server hosting. Below you will see the full story.

The original intent

As you most likely know, our original intent was to revive the Legacy Video Maker, the outdated flash version of Vyond's services, and here is where I find the first major problem with the project. Simply put, by reviving the Legacy Video Maker, we were partaking in, and endorsing piracy. Originally when I joined the team, I didn't think of it as such, but as time passed, I realized how morally and legally wrong it is.

Against Vyond

Believe it or not, our team has talked with some staff at Vyond, who had earlier asked us to take the project down, of which we complied. However, during a fiasco with a project using our source code known as GoAnimate Remastered, a private archive of the repo was leaked. Our delay in taking down the repo was mostly due to the fact that we were waiting on VisualPlugin to make a public announcement about our plans, which has taken longer then originally expected, but this isn't an excuse, and we are truly sorry to Vyond for taking as long as it has to take down the repo.

The future of GoAnimate Wrapper

There is none. We have no plans to continue or use the GoAnimate Wrapper identity, and all of us have since moved on. Continuing to operate GoAnimate Wrapper has not only risked us legal trouble, but has also generally hurt our reputation, as being associated with a piracy tool isn't exactly the best image for anyone.

We also plan to be on the look out for clones, and explain to them the legal trouble the wrapper can bring them in (if we find any).

Thank you for reading our whole article. Now, go on and use a legal animation program.
